System Overview

The EcoSmart Garden is an automated Internet of Things (IoT) solution designed to eliminate the guesswork from plant care. By continuously analyzing soil moisture and ambient climate conditions, the system intelligently triggers a water pump only when the plant genuinely needs it, bridging the gap between nature and digital infrastructure.

Core Advantages

  • Water Conservation: Eliminates overwatering by acting purely on data logic.
  • Root Health: Ensures soil has appropriate dry periods before re-triggering the pump.
  • Global Telemetry: Monitor garden health and microclimate from anywhere via the cloud.

Technology Stack

ESP32 Microcontroller Capacitive Moisture Sensor DHT11 Climate Sensor 5V Relay Module Blynk IoT Cloud Tailwind CSS & Vanilla JS

Future Recommendations

  • Weather API Integration: Pause watering if local weather forecasts predict rain within 12 hours.
  • Solar Integration: Power the ESP32 and 12V pump entirely via a small solar panel and TP4056 battery circuit.
  • Machine Learning: Add an ESP32-CAM to visually detect early signs of leaf disease or weed growth.
